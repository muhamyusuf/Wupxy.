## Introduction

Satu.Rasa\_ is a profil page containing information about the owner and the owner's work. This page is made to showcase the owner's work and to make it easier for people to contact the owner. This page is made with the aim of increasing the owner's popularity and increasing the owner's income. The owner job is a freelance <b>photographer</b>.

## Features

- Gallery to showcase their work
- Animation for increase user experience
- Contact with the owner
- Responsive design
